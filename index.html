More info on our product

Tell us what you need

Just give us all the inputs! Our engine searches for the best matches for you basing on your location, age, price range and sport and leisure. Moreover, we'll inform you about weather conditions. For now, only Tricity attractions are available but soon we'll expand to whole Poland. Just give us all the inputs! First of all you would be asked about few features describing your dreamed holiday (location, sport or leisure, price, etc.). Moreover, we'll inform you about weather conditions.

The list of offers

We'll give you the list of offers for your search criteria. You can compare them basing on the price, accessibility or other criteria. In the next step we would give you a list of best matches basing on your preferences. You would take a look on the list and choose few of propositions to compare them basing on the criteria which are most important for you.
