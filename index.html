App features explained

Tell us what you need

Just give us all the inputs! Our engine searches for the best matches for you basing on your location, age, price range and sport and leisure. Moreover, we'll inform you about weather conditions. For now, only Tricity attractions are available but soon we'll expand to whole Poland. Just give us all the inputs! First of all you would be asked about few features describing your dreamed holiday (location, sport or leisure, price, etc.). Moreover, we'll inform you about weather conditions.

The list of offers

We'll give you the list of offers for your search criteria. You can compare them basing on the price, accessibility or other criteria. In the next step we would give you a list of best matches basing on your preferences. You would take a look on the list and choose a few of our propositions, to compare them basing on the criteria which are the most important for you.

Ranking basing on your choices

xxxxxThen we would show you a ranking with a few of propositions and you will be able to decide where to go!

Doubts? Contact the providers directly

We support you with the contact data of the providers listed on our search engine. Thanks to that you can quickly obtain all the extra info you need or simply make a reservation.
